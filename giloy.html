Tinospora Cordifolia (Giloy): The Ultimate Immunity Booster

Giloy, scientifically known as Tinospora Cordifolia, is deeply rooted in Ayurvedic science. In Sanskrit, it is called "Amrita," which translates to the root of immortality, owing to its abundant medicinal properties.

Benefits of Giloy

  • Boosts Immunity: Giloy is an antioxidant powerhouse that fights free radicals and prevents diseases.
  • Treats Chronic Fever: It is anti-pyretic in nature and can reduce signs of life-threatening fevers like Dengue and Malaria.
  • Improves Digestion: Regular consumption of Giloy powder with Amla helps in regulating bowel movements.
  • Reduces Stress and Anxiety: It acts as an adaptogenic herb and helps calm the mind.

How to Consume Giloy

The best way to consume Giloy is in the form of juice or Kadha (decoction). You can boil Giloy stems in water until it reduces to half, and consume it daily on an empty stomach for maximum benefits.
